## Project Overview  
This capstone project is a **web-based data visualization** focused on illustrating how climate change will affect an individual’s lifestyle over time.  
Users will input personalized metrics—such as what they eat, where they live, and other lifestyle factors—and the system will show projected impacts over the next **10, 20, and 50 years**.
